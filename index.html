The Maison

A single drop, suspended — then the slow, deliberate collapse into form. Every Oriel composition begins this way: with a moment of stillness before the bloom.

Founded in the perfumer's quarter of Grasse, Maison Oriel works in small, deliberate batches. No two compositions share a base. No two seasons share a formula.

We source absolutes directly from the growers we know by name — bergamot from Reggio Calabria, jasmine from the hills of Grasse, oud from sustainable agarwood plantations in Assam.

Bottled by hand. Numbered. Signed.

The Signature

Ambre Solaire

A study in warmth — bergamot, amber, and the slow patience of Madagascan vanilla.

Three years in composition. Aged six months in glass before bottling. The flagship of the Maison and the scent the atelier returns to when the light begins to fail.
